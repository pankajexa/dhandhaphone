## Quick Rate Lookup Tips

1. **Unbranded + unpackaged food** is almost always 0%
2. **Same food branded + packaged** jumps to 5% or 12%
3. **Clothing under Rs 1000** = 5%, above Rs 1000 = 12%
4. **If in doubt**, most manufactured goods are 18%
5. **Check HSN code** on the GST portal for exact rate: cbic-gst.gov.in
6. **Composite supply** takes the rate of the principal supply
