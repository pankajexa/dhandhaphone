# GST Overview

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is India's unified indirect tax, replacing VAT, service tax, excise, and others. Launched July 1, 2017.

## Five Rate Tiers

| Rate | Category | Examples |
|------|----------|----------|
| 0% | Essential | Fresh food, milk, fruits, vegetables, grains |
| 5% | Basic necessities | Packaged food, tea, edible oil, spices |
| 12% | Standard (lower) | Processed food, mobile phones, sewing machines |
| 18% | Standard | Most manufactured goods, electronics, services |
| 28% | Luxury/demerit | Cars, cement, AC, aerated drinks, tobacco |

Additional cess applies on some 28% items (cars, tobacco, coal).

## Who Must Register

**Mandatory registration if:**
- Annual turnover exceeds **Rs 40 lakh** (goods) in normal states
- Annual turnover exceeds **Rs 20 lakh** (goods) in special category states (NE states, Himachal, Uttarakhand, J&K)
- Annual turnover exceeds **Rs 20 lakh** (services) in normal states
- Annual turnover exceeds **Rs 10 lakh** (services) in special category states
- Inter-state supply of any amount
- E-commerce sellers (no threshold)
- Casual taxable persons

## GSTIN Format

15-character alphanumeric code:
```
22AAAAA0000A1Z5
│  │         │││
│  │         ││└─ Check digit
│  │         │└── Default "Z"
│  │         └─── Entity number (1-9, A-Z)
│  └─────────────  PAN (10 chars)
└────────────────  State code (01-37)
```

## Basic Compliance Requirements

1. **Issue GST invoices** for all taxable supplies
2. **File monthly/quarterly returns** (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B)
3. **Pay tax** by the 20th of the following month
4. **Maintain records** for 6 years (72 months) from filing date
5. **Annual return** (GSTR-9) by December 31 of following year
6. **Display GSTIN** at place of business and on invoices

## Voluntary Registration

Even below threshold, registration is useful if:
- You sell on e-commerce platforms (mandatory)
- You want to claim Input Tax Credit
- Your customers need GST invoices
- You do inter-state business
